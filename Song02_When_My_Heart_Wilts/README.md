# When My Heart Wilts - என் இருதயம் தொய்ந்து போகும் போதெல்லாம்

This song of tender longing and childlike trust echoes Psalm 61 — “Hear my cry, O God… From the end of the earth I will cry to You, when my heart is overwhelmed; lead me to the rock that is higher than I… I will abide in Your tabernacle forever; I will trust in the shelter of Your wings.” It is a cry from the ends of the earth, when the heart faints, seeking refuge in the higher Rock and under His wings. In the eternal day, this longing is fulfilled — no more fainting hearts, only eternal abiding in God's presence (Revelation 21:3).
Closing Prayer for the Listener
Lord Jesus Christ, our higher Rock and sheltering wings,
thank You for hearing our cry when our hearts faint.
Lead us to Yourself, hide us under Your wings.
In the eternal day, let us abide in Your tabernacle forever.
Come quickly, our Refuge and Strength.
