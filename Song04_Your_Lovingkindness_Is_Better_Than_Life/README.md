# Your Lovingkindness Is Better Than Life - ஜீவனைப்பார்க்கிலும் உம் கிருபை நல்லது

This song of deep thirst and joyful satisfaction echoes Psalm 63 — “O God, You are my God; earnestly I seek You; my soul thirsts for You… Because Your steadfast love is better than life, my lips will praise You… My soul will be satisfied as with fat and rich food.” It is a declaration that God's lovingkindness surpasses life itself, leading to praise in every moment. In the eternal day, this thirst is fully quenched — no more dry land, only eternal satisfaction in the presence of the Lamb (Revelation 22:1–5).
Closing Prayer for the Listener
Lord Jesus Christ, better than life itself,
thank You for Your lovingkindness that satisfies our deepest thirst.
Let our souls seek You early, praise You always.
In the eternal day, let us drink from Your river of life forever.
Come quickly, our Satisfaction and Joy.
