# O God, You Have Cast Us Off - தேவா நீர் எங்களைக் கைவிட்டீர்

This song of lament and plea for restoration echoes Psalm 60 — “O God, You have rejected us, broken our defenses; You have been angry; oh, restore us! … Give us help from trouble, for the help of man is useless. Through God we will do valiantly, for it is He who shall tread down our enemies.” It cries out in apparent abandonment while claiming God's promises of victory and restoration. In the eternal day, this plea is answered — no more scattering, only eternal unity in the New Jerusalem with the Lamb (Revelation 21:3).
Closing Prayer for the Listener
Lord Jesus Christ, faithful God who restores the scattered,
thank You for Your anger that lasts a moment, but Your mercy endures forever.
Return to us, restore us, give help from trouble.
In the eternal day, let us dwell in Your presence forever.
Come quickly, our Restorer and King.
