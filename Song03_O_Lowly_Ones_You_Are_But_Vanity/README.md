# O Lowly Ones, You Are But Vanity - கீழ்மக்களே நீங்கள் மாயை

This song of sober warning and call to exclusive trust echoes Psalm 62 — “For God alone my soul waits in silence; from Him comes my salvation… Those of low estate are but a breath; those of high estate are a delusion; in the balances they go up; together they are lighter than a breath. Put no trust in extortion; set no vain hopes on robbery; if riches increase, set not your heart on them.” It confronts the vanity of man and calls all to rest in God alone. In the eternal day, this trust is perfected — no more vanity or oppression, only eternal rest in God's faithfulness (Revelation 21:3–4).
Closing Prayer for the Listener
Lord Jesus Christ, our only sure foundation and refuge,
thank You for reminding us that man is but vanity and riches fade.
Let us pour out our souls to You alone, trust in Your salvation.
In the eternal day, let us rest in Your presence without fear.
Come quickly, our Rock and Salvation.
