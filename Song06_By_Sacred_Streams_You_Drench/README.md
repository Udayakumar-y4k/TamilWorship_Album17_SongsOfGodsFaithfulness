# By Sacred Streams You Drench - தேவநதியினால் நீர்ப்பாய்ச்சுகிறீர்

This song of urgent cry and confident hiding echoes Psalm 64 — “Hear my voice, O God, in my complaint; preserve my life from dread of the enemy. Hide me from the secret plots of the wicked… But God shoots His arrow at them; they are wounded suddenly… The righteous will rejoice in the Lord and take refuge in Him.” It is a plea for protection from hidden evil while trusting God's sudden justice. In the eternal day, this hiding is perfected — no more secret plots, only eternal refuge in God's presence (Revelation 7:15).
Closing Prayer for the Listener
Lord Jesus Christ, who hears our voice in prayer and hides us from evil,
thank You for shielding us from secret plots and sudden attacks.
Hear our cries, shoot Your arrows at the wicked, let the righteous rejoice.
In the eternal day, let us take refuge in You forever.
Come quickly, our Hiding Place and Deliverer.
